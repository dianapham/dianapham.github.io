Adorbz

Apr 22nd, 2014



Aren’t these just adorable?

I’m really glad I randomly started doing pixel art again. It makes me feel like I have a chance at making that game I wanted to make years ago.

I wish when I had more support when I was younger to do things that I wanted. I aspired to do some many things with technology. I really wanted to get into game development, but I had no idea how to go about it. It sucks I wasn’t persistant about it. Iono. I understand why I stopped trying though. I was tired of people using me. I knew the better I got at doing things like photo editing, the more people would make me do stuff for them.

My mom would always wake me up at 5 or 6 in the morning and make me edit pictures for her. It was really annoying. I’d have to clean up the cat’s poop and then go work for my mom. I remember always being really hungry.

When our computers started glitching out, I kinda just left it. I didn’t care anymore. I didn’t want to work for her anymore. Plus, she already told me to not get into computer work. So like, whatever right?

Meh.

It’s hard to be indifferent. I get so bitter and angry. I don’t like it when I’m like that. So I become indifferent. But that in turn comes with a price.

MEH.

Whatever. I can still do what I want. I’m stuck there anymore.

On a brighter not, I finally cut my fingernails. Heyoo!

I’m glad I met my boyfriend and then his friends. They’re all in game dev and it’s just a really nice community to be associated with. I’m like, going full circle into what I used to want to do. I guess now it’s still what I wanna do. So yeah. YEAH.
